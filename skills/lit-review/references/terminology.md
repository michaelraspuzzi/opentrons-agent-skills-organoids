# Cardiac Organoid Terminology

## Cell Types

### Primary Cells
- **iPSC**: Induced pluripotent stem cell (starting material)
- **hESC**: Human embryonic stem cell (alternative starting material)
- **CM / cardiomyocyte**: Contractile heart muscle cell
- **CF / cardiac fibroblast**: Structural support, ECM production
- **EC / endothelial cell**: Vascular lining cells
- **SMC / smooth muscle cell**: Vascular wall cells
- **EpiC / epicardial cell**: Outer heart surface cells

### Progenitor Stages
- **Cardiac progenitor cell (CPC)**: NKX2.5+ precursor
- **First heart field (FHF)**: Left ventricle precursors
- **Second heart field (SHF)**: Right ventricle/outflow tract precursors
- **Proepicardium**: Epicardial precursors

## Differentiation Markers

### Pluripotency (should disappear)
- OCT4, NANOG, SOX2, TRA-1-60

### Mesoderm (early differentiation)
- T/Brachyury, MESP1, KDR

### Cardiac progenitor
- NKX2.5, ISL1, TBX5, GATA4

### Cardiomyocyte
- **Structural**: cTnT (TNNT2), cTnI, MYH6, MYH7, ACTN2 (α-actinin)
- **Ion channels**: SCN5A, KCNJ2, KCNH2
- **Calcium handling**: RYR2, SERCA2a (ATP2A2), PLN

### Maturity Markers
- **Immature**: MYH6 dominant, glycolytic metabolism
- **Mature**: MYH7 dominant, oxidative metabolism, T-tubules present

## Structural Features

### Sarcomere Organization
- **Z-disc**: α-actinin, desmin boundaries
- **I-band**: Thin filaments (actin)
- **A-band**: Thick filaments (myosin)
- **M-line**: Myomesin
- **Sarcomere length**: ~1.8-2.2 μm (mature)

### Ultrastructure
- **T-tubules**: Membrane invaginations for calcium signaling
- **Intercalated discs**: Cell-cell junctions (connexin-43)
- **Mitochondria**: 30-40% cell volume in mature CMs

## Functional Readouts

### Electrophysiology
- **Action potential duration (APD)**: Time from depolarization to repolarization
- **Upstroke velocity (dV/dt)**: Depolarization speed
- **Resting membrane potential (RMP)**: ~-80 to -90 mV (mature)
- **Conduction velocity**: Signal propagation speed

### Calcium Handling
- **Calcium transient amplitude**: Peak calcium during contraction
- **Rise time**: Time to peak calcium
- **Decay time (τ)**: Calcium removal rate
- **Diastolic calcium**: Resting calcium level

### Contractile Function
- **Beating rate**: Spontaneous contractions per minute
- **Beat regularity**: Coefficient of variation
- **Contraction velocity**: Speed of shortening
- **Force generation**: Measured in engineered tissues

### Metabolic Markers
- **Fatty acid oxidation (FAO)**: Mature CM energy source
- **Glycolysis**: Immature CM energy source
- **Oxygen consumption rate (OCR)**: Mitochondrial function
- **Extracellular acidification rate (ECAR)**: Glycolytic flux

## Organoid-Specific Terms

### Structure Types
- **Cardiac spheroid**: Simple 3D CM aggregate
- **Cardiac organoid**: Multi-cell type 3D structure
- **Cardioid**: Self-organizing chamber-forming organoid
- **Heart-on-chip**: Engineered tissue with perfusion
- **Cardiac microtissue**: Small engineered construct

### Formation Methods
- **Embryoid body (EB)**: Spontaneous aggregation
- **Forced aggregation**: Centrifugation into wells
- **Scaffold-free**: Cells self-organize
- **Scaffold-based**: Hydrogel or matrix support
- **Hanging drop**: Gravity-driven aggregation

### Maturation Approaches
- **Electrical pacing**: Applied electrical stimulation
- **Mechanical stretch**: Cyclic strain application
- **Metabolic maturation**: Fatty acid supplementation
- **Hormonal**: T3 thyroid hormone, dexamethasone
- **Long-term culture**: Extended culture duration

## Drug Response Terminology

### Cardiotoxicity Endpoints
- **QT prolongation**: Delayed repolarization
- **Arrhythmia**: Irregular beating patterns
- **Contractile dysfunction**: Reduced force/velocity
- **Structural damage**: Sarcomere disorganization

### Standard Compounds
- **Positive inotrope**: Isoproterenol (increases contractility)
- **Negative inotrope**: Verapamil (decreases contractility)
- **hERG blocker**: E-4031, dofetilide (QT prolongation)
- **Chronotrope**: Affects beating rate
